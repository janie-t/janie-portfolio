Kia Ora. Ko Janie taku ingoa. I code javascript webapps & websites. I became a developer for the love of learning and helping people through technology.

Ko Wai Au?


I have been teaching Digital Technology in Secondary Schools for 15 years. That is where my love of coding began. I had a 'light-bulb' moment one day, where I realised I wanted to be learning again too. I quit teaching in 2016 to do the Enspiral Dev Academy course in Wellington. This gave me the confidence and tools to begin my web development journey. After graduating I started a software development company with two other grads, called 'Manu'. This year has been the best year of my life; I have immersed myself in javascript and learned so much about the web. Every day I feel like a kid in a sandpit - playing with toys and building fun stuff.
