Kia ora! I'm Janie...

I live in Lower Hutt with my two tamariki, my partner, dog and chickens. I was a secondary school teacher for 15 years and loved teaching digital technology. I left that in 2016 to study with Enspiral Dev Academy. I like vintage stuff (such as cute teacups!). I also try my hand at photography - have a look at my Dogs of Petone Beach facebook page (if you like dogs too).

This year I started a software development company called 'Manu' with two other women. We established this company with the goal of creating products we are passionate about. We also want to improve diversity within the IT industry and contribute towards the growth of IT within Wellington and New Zealand. We aim to do this through contributing to open source code, collaboration within the industry and by creating a mentoring and learning incubator for young developers. We are currently looking for contracting opportunities as well as developing our own products.
